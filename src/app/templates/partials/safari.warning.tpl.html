Downloading

Safari does not fully support downloading encrypted attachments.

Your attachments are secure and encrypted, but to download them requires extra work. We recommend using a different web browser such as Chrome or Firefox for a better user experience.

To download attachments in Safari:

  1. Click your attachment once to decrypt it. Once decryption finishes you will see a download arrow icon appear.
  2. Right-click on your attachment and choose 'Download linked file as...'
  3. You will be prompted to save the attachment.
  4. Name your attachment from 'unknown' to the correct name, including the file extension.
